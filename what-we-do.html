The transnational nature of many businesses today exposes corporations and traveling employees to risks which are often unforeseen and/or misunderstood and therefore mismanaged owing to the unfamiliar environment of the country in which they occur. Increasingly international business seeks to maintain an enhanced level of awareness of situations and events in countries where they have operations or to which their employees may be traveling.

While there is a wealth of information in the media and public domain, little of it is sourced and presented with risk mitigation in mind or in ways which will facilitate effective decision making, particularly in a crisis. Hill & Associates' local presence and knowledge allows us to present factual information with meaningful interpretation and analysis of events in order to assist clients identify, forecast, assess and understand changing risks and by so doing reduce their overall exposure and initiate appropriate risk mitigation measures to protect their assets.

Corporate Intelligence
Due Diligence services
The damage that results from entering into contractual arrangements with unsuitable business partners cannot be underestimated. Profiling the background, standing, ownership and reputation of potential business partners is an essential and cost-effective exercise. Our standard due diligence report focuses on business reputation and practices in the form of either a basic or a detailed check.
Vendor Auditing
This programme aims to ensure complete transparency in the supply chain.
    The objectives include:
  • Ensuring suppliers are legitimate and ethical;
  • Removing the possibility of bogus companies being set up;
  • Removing potential conflicts of interest, such as employee position in vendor company;
  • Preventing collusion between a company's employees and its vendors, such as kickbacks;
  • Checking fees/prices against industry norms.
Pre-employment Screening
Employment screening services are an essential process to safeguard a corporation from hiring persons who are either unqualified or of questionable integrity.
