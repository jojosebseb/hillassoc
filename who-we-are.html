Our Story
PT Hill Konsultan Indonesia (HKI) opened in 1996 to provide professional security consulting services to multinational companies operating in all business sectors across the archipelago. HKI assists clients protect their personnel, property as well as information and reputation through its four core service lines, that includes the provision of Risk Intelligence, Security Risk Management, Corporate Intelligence, Fraud Prevention and Integrity Risk.

In light of the forever changing dynamics and threat environment in Indonesia, ranging from the threat of terrorism, social unrest, crime, disease and natural disasters, HKI has over the years established a reputation for providing accurate, real-time information on a daily basis coupled with realistic and sensible advice through its risk reporting service and 24 x 7 operations centre. HKI's 24 x 7 Emergency Response Team is also on stand-by, ready to respond and deploy to any incident at a moment's notice.

Our Background
Founded in 1992, Hill & Associates has become one of the world's leading risk mitigation consultancies while maintaining a uniquely Asian focus. We are headquartered in Hong Kong and have a comprehensive network of wholly-owned subsidiaries. By combining the expertise of our world-class professionals with our deep local knowledge, we are able to assist our clients in operating safely, efficiently and without disruption in some of the world's most difficult markets. We address a wide range of business risks and develop comprehensive solutions for both prevention and response.
Our Services
Our business activities are organised geographically but are overlaid by four (4) service lines, staffed by our most highly qualified operating executives who have responsibility for the consistency and quality of service delivery throughout the company as well as the ongoing development of new concepts and services. The four service lines are as follows:
  • Risk Intelligence
  • Security Risk Management
  • Corporate Intelligence
  • Fraud Prevention & Integrity Risk
